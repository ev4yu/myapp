Understanding Global Diabetes Trends

Diabetes affects millions worldwide, but its impact varies across regions and populations. These visualizations help us understand how diabetes prevalence, diagnosis rates, and healthcare spending differ around the globe, revealing important patterns in this growing health challenge.

Sankey Diagram: Diabetes Risk Factors

What This Shows

  • Visualizes how diabetes distribution varies by sex and age groups
  • Reveals which demographic groups face higher diabetes risk
  • Line width indicates proportion of people in each category

Key Insights

These visualizations reveal important global patterns: Western Pacific regions show high prevalence, many African nations have high undiagnosed rates, and spending varies dramatically between developed and developing countries. Understanding these patterns helps target interventions where they're needed most.
